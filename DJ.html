x1aoy3

Bio

Born in the year of the Rabbit, but with the looks of a cat, x1aoy3 is a DJ who knows how to tame the beats. Hailing from Club Knowless, a mysterious Asian underground party label in the northwest hemisphere, x1aoy3 is driven by a desire to break down the binary opposition between flow and stasis by rearranging the elements of wind, rain, thunder, lightning, and fire.

Currently, x1aoy3 serves as the new resident host for Hong Kong Community Radio (HKCR), operating a monthly program called "3 Rabbit Holes." With her established presence in HKCR, she continues to bring new and diverse musical styles to her listeners.
